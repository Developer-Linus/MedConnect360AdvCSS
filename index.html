About Us

MedConnect 360 was created to solve the critical healthcare challenges faced by remote communities in Africa. With a shortage of healthcare professionals and long distances to the nearest health centers, we provide a platform that delivers quality healthcare through virtual consultations and appointment booking systems, making healthcare accessible to all.
